Security

Your security is our priority. At Tiffin Fusion, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe and secure experience.

1. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These measures include:

  • Data Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Access Control: Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Secure Hosting: Our servers are hosted in secure data centers with robust physical and digital protections.

2. User Responsibilities

To help us protect your information, you should:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for your accounts.
  • Regularly update your software and devices to protect against security threats.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information unless necessary and ensure you are communicating through secure channels.
  • Notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.
